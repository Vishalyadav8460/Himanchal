Only When The British Established Their Chain Of Hill Stations Dis TourismReceive Recognition In The State. Tourism Activity Received A Shot In The Arm When British Declared Shimla "The Summer Capital Of India" In 1864. Post Independence , More Investment In The InfraStructure Sector Led To Openting Of Tourism In State. However The Biggest Boost ToTousism Occured In The Mid 80s And 90s.
